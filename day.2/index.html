Shakib Al Hasan is a Bangladeshi cricketer and current captain of the Bangladesh national cricket team in Tests and T20Is. He is widely considered to be the country's greatest sportsman. He is known for his aggressive left-handed batting style in the middle order and controlled slow left-arm orthodox bowling.
